Loop Breaker

Stop rewriting the same unfinished tasks every week.

Paste or photograph your last few weekly to-do lists. Loop Breaker finds the tasks that keep reappearing and makes you decide on each one: commit, schedule, or kill. It saves your board, rechecks it on its own every week, and can email you the verdict.

1

Add your weeks. Paste each week's to-do list, one task per line.

2

Analyze. It compares the weeks and finds what keeps repeating.

3

Decide. Every recurring task gets a blunt verdict and a next step.

Commit, needs a first step Schedule, needs a time Kill, let it go

Get confronted every week. Loop Breaker re-checks this board on its own every Monday. Add your email and it will send you the verdict without you lifting a finger.
